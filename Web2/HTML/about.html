OUR HISTORY

AO Motors was founded in 2005 to respond to the needs in the industry and facilitate the travel experience. Our organization started with a vision that prioritizes dynamism in the sector and customer satisfaction. Since then, we have continued our mission of providing excellent service to our customers at every stage. We initially started operating with a limited vehicle fleet and at a local level. However, we have continuously improved in line with our customer satisfaction-oriented approach and rapidly changing industry demands. In the first years, unlike other companies in the sector, we expanded our vehicle categories to offer more options to our customers and took care to provide services at affordable prices. In 2008, we discovered the power of the internet and started to provide services on the online platform. This has allowed us to offer our customers an easier, faster and more accessible booking experience. With the development of technology, we have taken steps to make our customers' travel plans even easier by investing in mobile applications and online reservation systems. By adapting to the changing needs in the industry, we adopted a sustainability-focused approach in 2015. We have taken steps towards becoming an environmentally friendly company with the environmentally friendly vehicles and green practices we added to our fleet. Today, we have a large fleet of vehicles and serve many locations across the country. While maintaining our goal of being a pioneer in the industry with innovative solutions, we continue to keep our customers' satisfaction at the highest level. In the future, we aim to follow technological innovations, create a more comprehensive service network, grow based on sustainability and customer satisfaction, and always improve the travel experience.
